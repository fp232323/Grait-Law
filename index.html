Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any technical knowledge to use these AI systems?
Not at all. Grait Law handles every technical aspect — setup, integration, configuration, and ongoing maintenance. We train your staff with simple, role-specific workflows. If you can use email, you can use the AI tools we implement.
How long does implementation take?
Tier 1 implementations typically take 1–2 weeks. Tier 2 takes 3–4 weeks. Tier 3 Enterprise implementations are scoped individually but typically run 4–8 weeks. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption.
Are the AI tools compliant with bar ethics rules?
Yes. We exclusively partner with platforms designed for legal use — they maintain attorney-client privilege protections, data encryption, and are purpose-built to meet bar association ethics guidelines. Every platform we recommend has been vetted for legal compliance. For Tier 3, we also conduct dedicated AI governance and compliance auditing.
Can I start with one tier and upgrade later?
Absolutely. Our tiers are designed as a progression. Many firms start with Tier 1 to prove ROI, then upgrade to Tier 2 within 3–6 months. When you upgrade, your existing setup carries forward — you only pay the incremental difference in setup costs.
What happens if an AI tool produces an error or hallucination?
We use citation-verified platforms (Lexis+ AI, Clearbrief) specifically designed to minimize hallucinations in legal contexts. Additionally, our training covers how attorneys should review AI output — AI is a first-draft and research accelerant, not a replacement for attorney judgment. Our monthly audits catch and correct any drift.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
We ask for a 3-month initial commitment to allow sufficient time to demonstrate ROI. After that, the engagement is month-to-month. We're confident in the value we deliver — you stay because the results speak for themselves, not because of a contract.
Is there an email where I can reach out directly to someone at Grait Law?
Feel free to reach out by email to fred@graitlaw.com.
What countries does Grait Law serve?
While Grait Law is headquartered in Las Vegas, Nevada (NV), United States of America (USA), Grait Law's services can benefit law firms in most countries. This is because the efficiencies created are universal. The only thing that would change would be the applicable law and whether that portion of the services can be offered by our case law partners.
